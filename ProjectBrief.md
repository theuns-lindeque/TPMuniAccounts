Project Briefing: Trueprop Municipal Utilities Analytics App1. Project OverviewA specialized, AI-driven web application designed to automate the analysis of municipal utility accounts against tenant recoveries across the Trueprop commercial and student accommodation portfolio. The system will ingest municipal bills and recovery files, perform historical trend analysis, and automatically surface financial risks, recovery deficits, and usage anomalies.2. Scope & StakeholdersTarget Portfolio: Stellenbosch properties including Nassau, Plumbago, Armentum, 39 on Plein, Ivy League 1, and Ivy League 2.Operational Stakeholders: The platform is designed to streamline workflows for the property management and administration team (Lauren, Britney), provide actionable data for facilities management (Tracey, alongside the on-the-ground team like Clayton and Lukas), and deliver high-level financial oversight.3. Core ObjectivesAutomated Data Ingestion: Frictionless upload and parsing of complex municipal PDFs and recovery CSVs.Real-Time Analytics & Performance Tracking: Instant visualization of municipal expense trends versus net recovery trends.Self-Learning AI Anomaly Detection: An evolving AI engine that flags risks (e.g., usage spikes, recovery shortfalls) and learns from user feedback (corrections/false positives) to improve future accuracy.4.
